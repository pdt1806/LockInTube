<h1 align="center">LockInTube</h1>

<p align="center">Clear all distractions from YouTube for people who need to lock in.</p>
<p align="center">(hint hint: This app will always be free and will never require you to pay any pennies!)</p>

## Features

- Hide all distractions from YouTube, including:
  - Recommended videos
  - Shorts
  - Comments
  - Sidebar videos
  - End screen videos
- Play Shorts as normal videos without the Shorts interface
- Customize your left sidebar with the necessary links
